The Destroyers is a punk band formed in 1998. Led by Mary Ann Anarchy, who formed the band, initially being only women. Then through time there were changes. They released their first two albums on independent Hellcat Records, owned by Rancid's Tim Armstrong before moving to Sire, part of Warner Music Group.

Mary Ann Anarchy, is a vocalist and guitarist, while Josh Motherf * cking Willow was the first guitarist. Wolf Von Fang was a bass player.

The Destroyers was included in the soundtrack of the video game for the xbox console Tony Hawk's Underground 2 with his single "Beat Your Heart Out" and in Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 4 with Seneca Falls.
